Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and you'll be prorated for any difference in cost.
What happens if I exceed my conversation limit?
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional conversation packs to avoid any service interruption.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start, and you can cancel at any time during the trial period.
What integrations are available?
We integrate with popular CRMs, help desk systems, email platforms, and social media channels. Professional and Business plans include all integrations.
